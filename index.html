    BEAST NETWORK TOPOLOGY

    ORGANIZATIONS INVOLVED IN THE NETWORK:

    The beast-dev network includes the following peer orgs,

    • Org-hospitala with peers – peer0, peer1, peer2 and peer3.
    • Org-hospitalb with peers – peer0, peer1, peer2 and peer3.
    • Org-patients with peers – peer0, peer1, peer2 and peer3 and
    • Org-govtregulator with peers – peer0 and peer1.

    And the orderer org,

    • Beast-ordorg with 3 ordering nodes - orderer1, orderer2 and orderer3.

    CHANNELS IN THE NETWORK AND THE MEMBERS IN THE CHANNEL:

    The network has the following channels,

    • Urus-beast-channel with the members org-hospitala, org-hospitalb, org-patients and org-govtregulator.
    • Beast-hospitala-channel with the members org-hospitala, org-patients and org-govtregulator.
    • Beast-hospitalb-channel with the members org-hospitalb, org-patients and org-govtregulator.
